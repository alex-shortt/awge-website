About cookies

Cookies are pieces of information a web site can send to your computer's browser for record-keeping purposes. AWGEshit.com uses cookies to ascertain which web pages are visited and how often, to make our web site more user friendly, and to give you a better experience when you return to the website. Most web browsers automatically accept cookies, but you do not have to accept them. To disable cookies, you should access the "help" tab on your browser or read the information that came with your browser software.
